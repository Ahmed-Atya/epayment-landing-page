About Us

GrailPay™ originates from the inspiration of 16-year-old William
Messina to find ways that common people could benefit from greater
efficiences in the way that consumers interact with retailers.

William Learned about how the costs of advertising
(customer acquisition) and transcation settlement (debit
and credit card processing) impact the costs of goods
which led to william creating the GrailPay™ retailer
savings that are converted to instant cashback to its usrers.

GrailPay™ provides the Retailer savings, which fund effective customer acquisition and retenation stratigies.

GrailPay™ can be deployed with as few as one
retailer but it expected to have at least one national
retailer featured from a few of the various retailer
segments such as, grocery,department store, home
repair, restaurants and travel ("founding retailer").
